Name Ether
Description Ether is the cryptocurrency of the Ethereum platform which is an open source, distributed computing platform that facilitates the development of decentralized apps and smart contracts. Ether is like the fuel for this platform which is still continuously developing. It is the world's second-largest cryptocurrency by market capitalization, only second to Bitcoin.

Name XRP
Description XRP is a cryptocurrency that was made by Ripple Labs, a US-based company. It is used for cross-border transactions at low cost. It is more cost-effective and has faster transaction times as compared to Bitcoin. Unlike Bitcoin and Ether, which use Proof of Work or Proof of Stake systems, XRP transactions have a consensus protocol, which means that the transaction times are shorter and less costly .It also has a total supply of 100 billion tokens.

Name Bitcoin
Description Bitcoin is the first and most widely recognized cryptocurrency, known to be created by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009. It is a decentralized digital currency, as it operates independently of any central bank or government. It uses blockchain technology for transactions and serves as a store of value for investors worldwide. There are currently 21 million coins, which is why it is called "digital gold."
